Fig and Arugula Salad

This salad with fresh figs and arugula is simple and so delicious!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups arugula
  • 8 fresh figs, quartered
  • Â¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons toasted pine nuts
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar

Steps

  1. Toss arugula, figs, Parmesan cheese, and pine nuts together in a large bowl.
  2. Drizzle honey and balsamic vinegar over salad before serving.
